Vibe-shot Guide

Master Vibe-shot to supercharge your workflow. Here’s a quick look at all the features.

Shortcuts & Core Workflow

  • Ctrl + Shift + Y → Start a capture. The image is instantly copied to your clipboard!
  • Ctrl + Shift + I → Open this popup window.
  • Esc → Cancel a capture in progress.

Managing Your Screenshots

Once you open the popup, you can manage your saved shots:

  • Copy: Copies the image to your clipboard again.
  • Remove: Deletes the screenshot permanently.
  • Rename (✏️): Click the pencil icon to give your shot a custom, memorable name.
  • Drag & Drop: Drag any screenshot from the popup directly to your desktop or an application to save it as a WebP file.

Header Controls

  • Copy & Remove Toggle: When active, using "Copy" or dragging the image will also automatically delete it from the list. Perfect for single-use shots.
  • Open in Window (↗): Opens this popup in a separate, persistent window so it doesn't close automatically.
  • Clear All Button: Deletes all saved screenshots at once. You'll be asked to confirm to prevent accidents.
